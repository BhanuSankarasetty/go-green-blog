Quick Actions to Go Green 💪

No effort is too small. These tiny steps today shape a greener tomorrow.

🚫 Say No to Plastic

Refuse single-use plastic. Carry a reusable bag, bottle, and utensils.

🌱 Plant a Tree

Plant a sapling in your community, school, or even a pot at home.

🚴 Use Green Transport

Walk, cycle, or use public transport instead of driving alone.

♻️ Segregate Waste

Separate dry, wet, and recyclable waste in your home daily.

💧 Save Water

Turn off taps, fix leaks, and use buckets instead of showers.

📱 Share Green Tips

Share sustainable hacks and tips with friends or on social media.

🌞 Use Natural Light

Keep lights off during the day and use sunlight as much as possible.

🛍️ Support Local Eco-Brands

Buy from local, sustainable, or organic businesses when possible.

📚 Educate Someone

Talk to a friend or family member about climate change and solutions.

🌐 Sign a Petition

Support an environmental cause by signing and sharing green petitions.

🧴 Go Natural

Switch to eco-friendly personal care and home cleaning products.

🗓️ Commit Weekly

Pick one green action to practice every week and stick with it.
